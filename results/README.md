## Experiment 2: Full-Scale Evasion Test

**File:** `exp2_results.json`

75,948 sequences: all 6,329 reviewed UniProt toxins × 6 mutation rates
(5–85%) + 37,974 matched benign negatives.

### Overall

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total sequences | 75,948 |
| Threat homologs | 37,974 |
| Benign negatives | 37,974 |
| Identity-to-reference AP | 0.9026 (1.8× baseline) |
| SVD severity AP | **0.9728** (1.9× baseline) |
| SVD improvement | +7.8% overall |

### By Sequence Identity — The Key Result

| Identity range | N | Identity AP | SVD AP | SVD lift |
|---------------|---|------------|--------|----------|
| 80–100% | 6,329 | High | High | — |
| 60–80% | 6,329 | Good | Strong | Positive |
| 40–60% | 6,329 | Moderate | Strong | Positive |
| **20–40%** | **6,329** | **0.6896** | **0.9076** | **+31.6%** |
| 0–20% | 6,329 | Low | Moderate | Positive |

> **At 20–40% sequence identity** (the AI-redesign evasion zone where
> ProteinMPNN operates at high sampling temperature): SVD severity
> outperforms identity-based scoring by **31.6%**.
